About Project

Flood Prediction

Floods are among the most cruel and destructive acts of nature; causing damage to human life and property along with damaging the agricultural output and animal life alike.
Flooding occurs when an extreme volume of water is carried by rivers, creeks and many other geographical features.
The idea is to implement a robust model, culminating all the desired factors to predict a possibility of flooding in a particular area. The desired factors would be duly analyzed and the output would be generated with an increased amount of accuracy.

  • Live data of any location.
  • High Accuracy.
  • Analysis with diffrent techniques
  • Predicting the probability of flood.
